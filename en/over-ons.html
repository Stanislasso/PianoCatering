The Apulian Concept

The catering arm of Piano A & Piano B

Piano C is the catering arm of Piano in Leuven. At Piano A and Piano B you taste the authentic cuisine of Puglia, southern Italy, every day.

With Piano C, Alessandro, Andrea and Mattia bring those same recipes, ingredients and dishes to your event. No shortcuts, no substitutes.

Fresh dough, real ingredients and the hospitality of Salento, at your venue. Come taste in our restaurants first and build your catering menu with confidence.
