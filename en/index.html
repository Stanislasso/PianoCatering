The Apulian Concept

The catering arm of Piano A & Piano B

Piano C is the catering branch of Piano in Leuven. At Piano A and Piano B you taste the authentic cuisine of Puglia, southern Italy, every day. With Piano C, Alessandro, Andrea and Mattia bring those same recipes, ingredients and dishes to your event, no shortcuts, no substitutes.

Fresh dough, real ingredients and the hospitality of Salento, at your venue. Taste in our restaurants beforehand and plan your catering menu with confidence.
